# Career Crox – Calling Assistant

Career Crox Calling Assistant is a powerful mobile companion for the Career Crox CRM, designed to streamline the recruitment process by automating and managing high-volume calling workflows directly from your Android device.

## Key Features

### 🚀 Smart Calling Automation
*   **Instant CRM Sync:** Synchronize your calling queue directly from the CRM with a single tap.
*   **Automated Dialing Sequence:** Features a fixed 10-second timer between calls to give recruiters time to prepare while maintaining high momentum.
*   **Dual Mode Control:** Start, pause, or stop the calling sequence at any time from the mobile dashboard or via remote CRM commands.
*   **Manual Navigation:** Preview your queue and manually skip or go back to specific candidates when needed.

### 📊 Real-Time Performance Tracking
*   **Live Metrics:** Track daily stats including total Dialed calls, Connected calls, Not Connected calls, and total Talktime.
*   **Average Handling Time (AHT):** Automatically calculates your efficiency to help maintain recruitment standards.
*   **Call History Sync:** Automatically syncs incoming and missed call logs back to the CRM for complete record-keeping.

### 📁 Document & Recording Management
*   **One-Tap Profile Access:** Send a request to open the current candidate's full profile on your desktop CRM instantly.
*   **Resume Uploads:** Upload candidate resumes (PDF, Word, Images) directly from your phone to the CRM.
*   **Call Recording Integration:** Link and upload audio recordings of calls to specific candidate profiles for quality review.

### 📝 Recruitment Productivity Tools
*   **Smart Feedback:** Add call notes and feedback immediately after a call ends using customizable presets (e.g., Interested, Busy, Call Back).
*   **WhatsApp Integration:** Quickly initiate WhatsApp conversations with candidates without saving their numbers to your contacts.
*   **Task Management:** View today's tasks and overdue work items directly on your mobile device.
*   **Instant Reminders:** Receive notifications and reminders for high-priority recruitment activities.

### 🔒 Secure Pairing
*   **Device Pairing:** Securely connect your mobile device to the Career Crox CRM using a 6-digit pairing code.
*   **Remote Logout:** Ability to reset pairing or logout all sessions remotely for enhanced security.
*   **CRM Session Validation:** Automatically validates your CRM login status to ensure data security.
