About the App

Transform Your Recruitment Workflow with Smart Calling Automation

Streamline your recruitment process with Career Crox – Calling Assistant, the ultimate mobile companion for the Career Crox CRM. Designed specifically for recruitment professionals and high-volume callers, this app automates your outreach, manages candidate interactions, and keeps your CRM data perfectly in sync—all from the palm of your hand.

🚀 Smart Calling Automation

  • Instant CRM Sync: Synchronize your calling queue directly from the CRM with a single tap.
  • Automated Dialing Sequence: Maintain high momentum with a smart 10-second countdown between calls, giving you just enough time to prepare.
  • Dual Mode Control: Start, pause, or stop the calling sequence at any time from the mobile dashboard or via remote CRM commands.
  • Manual Navigation: Preview your queue and manually skip or revisit specific candidates when needed.

📊 Real-Time Performance Tracking

  • Live Metrics: Track daily stats including total Dialed calls, Connected calls, and total Talktime.
  • Efficiency Analytics: Automatically calculates your Average Handling Time (AHT) to help you maintain peak recruitment standards.
  • Call History Sync: Automatically syncs incoming and missed call logs back to the CRM for complete record-keeping.

📁 Document & Recording Management

  • One-Tap Profile Access: Send a request to open the current candidate's full profile on your desktop CRM instantly.
  • Resume Uploads: Upload candidate resumes (PDF, Word, Images) directly from your phone to the CRM.
  • Call Recording Integration: Link and upload audio recordings of calls to specific candidate profiles for quality review and training.

📝 Recruitment Productivity Tools

  • Smart Feedback: Add call notes immediately after a call ends using customizable presets (e.g., Interested, Busy, Call Back, Not Interested).
  • WhatsApp Integration: Quickly initiate WhatsApp conversations with candidates without saving their numbers to your personal contacts.
  • Task Management: View today's tasks and overdue work items directly on your mobile device.
  • Instant Reminders: Receive notifications for high-priority recruitment activities and follow-ups.

🔒 Secure & Seamless Connectivity

  • Secure Device Pairing: Connect your mobile device to the Career Crox CRM using a secure 6-digit pairing code.
  • Remote Management: Ability to reset pairing or logout all sessions remotely for enhanced security.
  • CRM Session Validation: Automatically validates your login status to ensure your recruitment data remains protected.

Permissions Note: Career Crox – Calling Assistant requires READ_CALL_LOG and CALL_PHONE permissions to automate the dialing process and ensure that call outcomes are accurately synced with your CRM, providing a seamless experience for professional recruiters.
